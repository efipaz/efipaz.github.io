On Travel Photography

Capturing moments of connection, beauty, and truth

"Photography is not about capturing what you see, but about revealing what you feel. When traveling through India and Myanmar, I was constantly moved by the depth of spirituality, the warmth of the people, and the timeless wisdom that permeates everyday life. These images are an attempt to share those moments of connection."

- Efi Paz
