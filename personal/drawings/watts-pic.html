"Coming from a strict european christian background, Alan Watts may have seemed to some an unlikely eastern mystical philosopher, but perhaps this is why his books meant so much to me, since I encountered them when I was outgrowing my own christian rigidity. Watts succeeded in starting many minds, including my own, on the taboo path of conceptual freedom, for which I'll always be grateful."

- Norm Breyfogle
