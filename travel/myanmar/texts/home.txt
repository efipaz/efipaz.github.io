<p>This site is made of images taken in Myanmar (Burma) during our trip there in September 2005.  </p>

<p>During a two week break we took from our visit in India, we flew to Burma. We had an excellent guide, Aung-Ming, who took us in a circular route, from Yangoon (Rangoon) to Tangoon, Kalau, Pindia Caves  with the 10,000 Buddhas, Inle Lake, Mandaley where we spent a day in a meditaion center, and attended the face washing ceremony early in the morning, and back to Bagan, Pa-Ya and yangoon.</p>
