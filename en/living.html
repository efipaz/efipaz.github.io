Living in Peace with Money

A course bridging economics and spirituality

About the Course

A unique course designed to bridge the material and spiritual worlds. How can one live a life of inner peace while still dealing with the economic needs of modern life?

The course offers practical tools for conscious money management while maintaining inner balance and peace. It combines insights from Eastern philosophy with practical principles for healthy financial management.

Money is not the enemy. Our relationship to it is what determines the quality of our lives.

Course Topics

Financial Awareness

Understanding our automatic patterns regarding money and developing awareness of financial habits.

Releasing Fears

Identifying and releasing fears and anxieties related to money and financial future.

Inner Balance

Finding a balance between material needs and spiritual values.

Practical Tools

Developing healthy financial habits that serve life, not the other way around.
