About Efi Paz

Efi Paz brings together diverse interests: spiritual philosophy, travel photography, business consulting, and exploration of consciousness.

With years of experience in business coaching and a deep interest in Eastern philosophy, particularly the teachings of Krishnamurti, Alan Watts, and Anthony de Mello, this website serves as a platform for sharing insights, images, and courses designed to help people live more consciously and meaningfully.
